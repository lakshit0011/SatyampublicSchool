We are school since 25 years

Satyam Public Senior Secondary School, established in 2000, is a reputed Hindi and English medium institution dedicated to quality education. The school offers classes from Play Group to Class 12th, providing streams in Arts, Commerce, and Science. With a focus on academic excellence, discipline, and moral values, Satyam Public School aims to nurture confident, responsible, and well-educated individuals ready to face future challenges successfully.
