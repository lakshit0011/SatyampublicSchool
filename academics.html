Admission Process

  • Fill out the online admission form through the official school website.
  • Submit all required documents including birth certificate and previous report card.
  • Attend the scheduled entrance test or interview as applicable.
  • Receive admission confirmation through email or phone.
  • Complete the fee payment process within the given timeline.
  • Collect the admission kit and welcome letter from the school office.
